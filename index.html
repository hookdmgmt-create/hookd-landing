What Hookd Will Be
◈
The Space

A dedicated creative facility in Central Arkansas housing a professional audio studio, a content studio built for photo and video, and flexible space for collaboration and events. Everything set up and ready — no teardown, no wasted time.

◈
What You Can Do Here

Record music. Film content. Shoot photography. Host podcasts. Work independently or bring in one of our engineers, photographers, or creative leads. Every service available à la carte.

◈
Membership

Hookd will offer monthly memberships at three tiers — giving you a set number of hours each month to use however you need. Members get priority booking, consistent access, and first consideration for opportunities within the Hookd network. Pricing shared with the interest list first.
