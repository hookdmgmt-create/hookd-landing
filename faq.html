The Basics

Hookd is a dedicated creative space in Central Arkansas where you can record music, film content, shoot photography, and host podcasts — all under one roof. It's designed for creators who need a real, professional space to do their work, whether that's once or every week.

Hookd will be located in Central Arkansas. We're currently finalizing our pilot location. People on the interest list will be the first to know the exact address when it's confirmed.

We're currently in Phase 1 — building our community and validating demand before committing to a permanent location. Our goal is to launch the pilot in 2026. The interest list gets first access to sessions when they open. See our full timeline →

Not yet. We're in early development — hosting Creator Nights, building our interest list, and working toward our pilot launch. Join the list to get notified the moment sessions open.

Using the Space

Hookd will have two dedicated studios — an audio studio for recording, mixing, and mastering, and a content studio for photo, video, and podcast production. The space is built for any creative discipline. See who Hookd is for →

Hookd operates on a BYOE (Bring Your Own Equipment) model — you can bring your own gear, or work with what's in the space. You're also welcome to bring your own engineer, photographer, or creative collaborator, or book one of ours.

Both. Hookd will offer hourly bookings for one-time sessions as well as monthly memberships for creators who want consistent access. Memberships get priority booking and better rates. More details on pricing will be shared with the interest list first.

Booking will be handled through our online system — pick your space, your time, and any add-ons like engineering or photography support. Everything is self-service and available from your phone.

Membership

Founding members are the first people to join Hookd when sessions open. They get first access to pilot passes, priority booking, and pricing before it's made public. Joining the interest list is how you get considered for founding membership.

Hookd will offer three membership tiers at different hour allotments per month. Exact pricing will be shared with the interest list first — before it goes public.

Memberships will have a short initial commitment period, then move to month-to-month. No long-term lock-in. Full terms will be shared when memberships open.

Getting Involved Now

Creator Nights are Hookd's community events — free gatherings for Central Arkansas creators to connect, collaborate, and experience what Hookd is building before it officially opens. Follow @hookdrecording for upcoming dates.

Joining puts you first in line. When Hookd launches, interest list members get first access to pilot passes, founding membership consideration, and pricing details — all before anything goes public. No commitment, no cost, opt out anytime.
